What’s a tontine ?

These are joint funds for which a deposit is tied to an expiry date chosen by the contributor. If the contributor does not withdraw its funds before the expiry date, the credit is lost for the depositor, and it is transfered to the pot. The credit is then used to acrude interest for all other depositors still in the game.

Volkstontine aims to provide all of the web 3 and cardano tech to enhance that old practice to make it bankless and autonomous.
